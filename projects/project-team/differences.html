If everyone is thinking alike, then somebody isn't thinking. George S. Patton

Becky has a Bachelor of Arts with majors in English and Economics. Both majors compliment her interest in programming - she considers programming to be both creative and logical, which is exactly what drew her to two different majors.

Seth has a Bachelors of Science with a major in Computer Science. He became interested in programming through board games. Seth found a love for programming during his first programming class. He enjoyed the idea of being able to create anything he wanted as long as he was willing to learn the process.

Seth is a man of action. He likes to take charge and solve problems as they come his way. Where some others like to sit and wait for directions, Seth likes to take the initiative and get things done.

Becky tends to be a big picture person who is interested in making processes more efficient wherever possible. In past jobs, she has taken the lead on big projects such as testing a newly built escrow software or creating title examination checklists for others to use.

Becky is the oldest child in her family, and she is, by her own admission, more cautious than her younger siblings. She believes this works to her advantage, though, because she sees potential issues far in advance and works to avoid them instead of fix them.

Seth is the youngest child in his family. He tends to be the more positive one in the family, always seeing the brighter side of things. Along with this optimism comes high aspirations. He has never felt like there is a goal he can not achieve as long as he is willing to put the effort into learning what it takes.
